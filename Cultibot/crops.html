Most Fertile Crops:

Tapioca
Tapioca is a starch extracted from the storage roots of the cassava plant.Cassava copes better with poor soils than many other food plants.
Banana
A banana is an edible fruit produced by several kinds of large herbaceous flowering plants.Tamil Nadu is the highest producer of bananas in the country.
Coconut
The coconut tree is a member of the palm tree family.Tamil Nadu is the highest producer of coconuts in the whole country.
Mango
A mango is an edible stone fruit produced by the tropical tree. The trees are long-lived, as some specimens still fruit after 300 years.
Rubber
The rubber tree will grow to heights of 100 to 130 feet, and can live up to 100 years. Its most famous feature is the milky white sap, known as latex.
Chikkoo
Chikoo Tree is a long-lived, everygreen tree native to Central and North America and well-known for its very sweet and flavorsome berry-like fruit.
Tea
The tea plant is an evergreen shrub that provides us with black, white, yellow, and green tea.The global tea market is worth about £40 billion.
Groundnut
The groundnut is a low growing annual plant. Being leguminous, it has the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen biologically into the soil.
Sugarcane
Sugarcane is a species of tall, perennial grass that is used for sugar production.The plants are 2–6 m tall with stout, fibrous stalks that are rich in sucrose.
